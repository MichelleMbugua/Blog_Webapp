Buttered and Blessed

Are you a university student looking for quick and easy meal ideas? Look no further! Here are some simple food recipes that are perfect for busy students.

1. One-Pot Pasta

This recipe is perfect for those who want a delicious meal without the hassle of multiple pots and pans. Simply combine pasta, your choice of vegetables, and a flavorful sauce in one pot. Cook until the pasta is tender and the sauce has thickened. It's a quick and satisfying meal that can be customized to your taste.

2. Chicken Fried Rice

This quick and easy recipe is perfect for busy students. Simply stir-fry cooked rice with your favorite vegetables and protein, then add soy sauce and other seasonings to taste. It's a delicious way to use up any leftover ingredients in your kitchen.

3. Sardine spaghetti

This recipe is perfect for those who want a quick and flavorful meal. Simply cook spaghetti according to package instructions, then toss it with canned sardines, garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes. It's a simple yet delicious dish that can be made in under 20 minutes.
